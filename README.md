# Complex Analysis · Interactive

**Analytic functions, contour integration, and conformal mappings**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 4 · Graduate

## What You'll Learn

- Complex numbers and analytic functions
- Cauchy-Riemann equations
- Complex integration and Cauchy's theorem
- Power series and Laurent series
- Residue calculus
- Conformal mappings and applications

## References

- Lars V. Ahlfors, *Complex Analysis* (3rd ed., McGraw-Hill)
- Elias M. Stein & Rami Shakarchi, *Complex Analysis* (Princeton Lectures in Analysis II)
- John B. Conway, *Functions of One Complex Variable* (2nd ed., GTM 11, Springer)
